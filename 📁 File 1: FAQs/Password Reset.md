# Password Reset

## Overview

If you have forgotten your password, follow the steps below to reset it securely.

## Steps

1. Visit the Login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Open the email and click the reset link.
6. Create a new strong password.
7. Log in with your new password.

## Common Issues

### I didn't receive the email

- Check your Spam or Junk folder.
- Wait 5–10 minutes.
- Verify that the email address is correct.
- Request another reset email.

### The reset link expired

Request a new password reset link.

## Contact Support

If the problem continues, contact Customer Support.
